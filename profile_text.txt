NR Intellectual Solution is an autonomous, multi-disciplinary consulting firm. It has been inaugurated by a group 
of highly qualified and dedicated experts of development interventions. It started it journey in the year 2022. NRIS 
is proud to be an evolvement, partner providing appropriate, adequate and sustainable solutions and resolution 
for infrastructure development through innovative, originative ideas, capacity building, and training. NR Intellectual 
Solution. provides services from the stage of inception to the completion of projects. Focusing on the need of 
clients and society, NR Intellectual Solution has expanded its activities in diversified fields to develop areas of 
expertise and strengthen its capabilities. 
NR Intellectual Solution. has earned a reputation for engineering excellence. NR Intellectual Solution is capable 
to provide the related, complete service portfolio ranging from Environmental monitoring and Evaluation, Baseline 
Survey, Initial Environmental Examination (IEE), Environmental Impact Assessment (EIA), Resettlement Action 
Plan (RAP) Study, Environmental Social Impact Assessment (ESIA), Socio Economic Feasibility Study, Strategic 
Environmental Assessment (SEA), Topographical Survey, Feasibility studies, Baseline Survey, Waste 
Management, Natural Resource Management and Agricultural Solution for various sectors such as 
Transportation, Power & Energy, Oil and Mining, Port & Estuary & Inland Navigation and so many various Sector.

     Principles & Strategies 
NR Intellectual Solution. thrives for sustainable integrated development by participating in planning and 
implementation of development interventions through consulting services in training, research and project related 
activities. NR Intellectual Solution. believes that in development efforts, participatory approach is very effective and 
sustainable; and experts give due considerations to local wisdom in all stages of project cycle. In its actions the 
firm adheres to the following principles: 
► Bear in mind the social and economic interests of the women and deprived section of the population 
► Be concerned with the effects of development work on people and environment and 
► Join hands with experts from different fields in a coordinated manner for sustenance. 
 
NR Intellectual Solution. is capable of making positive contributions in technological, social, economic, agronomic 
and environmental fields. The experts and professionals associated with it have remarkable records of quality 
services in these fields. It must be noted that while they have valuable experience of working in multi-disciplinary 
team of experts, they give due and added importance to people's participation in different stages of project 
development and execution.
